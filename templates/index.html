Te Wahi Auaha is a makerspace based at Takapuna Grammar School, Tāmaki Makaurau.

Open to students and staff, the vision of Te Wahi is to utilise new emerging technologies across different subject areas.

It's an open space with facilities for 3D printing, laser cutting, augmented reality, digital modeling and animation.
